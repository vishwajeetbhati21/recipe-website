Chocolate Cake

Medium ⏱️ 60 minutes 👥 8 Servings

Description

This is a moist and decadent chocolate cake that's perfect for birthdays, celebrations, or just when you're craving something sweet. The cake is rich, fudgy, and pairs perfectly with chocolate frosting. It looks impressive but is surprisingly easy to make!

Ingredients

For the Cake:

  • 200g All-purpose flour
  • 100g Cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoon Baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon Baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Salt
  • 200g Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 100ml Milk
  • 100ml Vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla extract
  • 150ml Hot water

For the Frosting:

  • 200g Butter, softened
  • 150g Cocoa powder
  • 300g Powdered sugar
  • 100ml Milk
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla extract

Instructions

Preparing the Cake:

  1. Preheat: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 8-inch round cake pans or one 9x13 inch pan.
  2. Mix dry ingredients: In a bowl, sift together flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Combine wet ingredients: In another bowl, whisk together sugar, eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  4. Combine: Pour wet ingredients into dry ingredients and mix until smooth. Stir in hot water - the batter will be thin, which is normal.
  5. Bake: Pour batter into prepared pans. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool: Let cakes cool in the pans for 15 minutes, then turn out onto wire racks to cool completely.

Preparing the Frosting:

  1. Cream butter: Beat softened butter in a bowl until light and fluffy (about 2 minutes).
  2. Add cocoa: Add cocoa powder and mix well.
  3. Add sugar: Gradually add powdered sugar while mixing, alternating with milk.
  4. Assemble: Place one cake layer on a serving plate, spread frosting on top. Add second layer and frost the entire cake.

💡 Tips

  • Use good quality cocoa powder for richer chocolate flavor.
  • Make sure eggs and butter are at room temperature for better mixing.
  • Don't overmix the batter - mix until just combined.
  • Let cakes cool completely before frosting to prevent frosting from melting.
  • You can add chocolate chips or chopped chocolate to the batter for extra richness.
  • Store in an airtight container for up to 3 days.
