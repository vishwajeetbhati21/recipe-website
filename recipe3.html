Chicken Tikka

Medium ⏱️ 45 minutes 👥 4 Servings

Description

Chicken Tikka is a popular Indian appetizer with tender pieces of chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, then cooked until slightly charred. It's flavorful, healthy, and perfect for serving at parties or as a main dish. This recipe can be made in an oven or on a grill.

Ingredients

  • 800g Chicken breast, cut into 1.5-inch cubes
  • 1 cup Plain yogurt
  • 3 tablespoon Ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 Green chilies, finely chopped
  • 3 tablespoon Lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoon Tikka masala powder or garam masala
  • 1 tablespoon Chili powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon Turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon Cumin powder
  • 2 tablespoon Oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Onions and lemon wedges for serving

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken: In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, lemon juice, tikka masala powder, chili powder, turmeric, cumin, oil, and salt. Add chicken pieces and coat well. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes (or up to 4 hours for better flavor).
  2. Preheat oven: Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Line a baking tray with aluminum foil and lightly grease it.
  3. Arrange on tray: Remove chicken from marinade and arrange pieces on the prepared baking tray in a single layer. You can reserve some marinade for brushing during cooking.
  4. Bake: Bake for 20-25 minutes, brushing with reserved marinade halfway through, until chicken is cooked through and slightly charred on edges.
  5. Check doneness: Make sure the chicken is cooked through - the meat should be white inside with no pink.
  6. Serve: Transfer to a serving platter. Garnish with fresh coriander, mint leaves, and serve with lemon wedges and sliced onions.

💡 Tips

  • Marinating overnight gives better flavor, but 30 minutes is the minimum.
  • Use boneless, skinless chicken breast for best results.
  • If using wooden skewers, soak them in water for 30 minutes before using to prevent burning.
  • You can grill the chicken instead of baking - cook over medium-high heat for 15-20 minutes.
  • Serve with mint chutney, onions, and lemon for authentic flavor.
  • Don't overcook - this can make the chicken dry. Check with a meat thermometer (165°F is perfect).
